Dashboard Insights & Breakdown

  • Top State: Maharashtra led the sales amount with 102K, followed closely by Madhya Pradesh (87K).
  • Preferred Payment Mode: COD (Cash on Delivery) was the most popular mode at 44%, followed by UPI at 21%.
  • Category Dominance: Clothing segment dominated the total quantity breakdown with 63%.
  • Top Customer: Harivansh contributed the highest purchase value of 9.9K.
  • Seasonality Trend: Monthly profits saw peak positive growth in the first half of the year, with a significant drop around August.
